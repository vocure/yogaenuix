About
Us

Nestled in a peaceful natural setting, Enuix is a holistic yoga retreat that offers a special fusion of yoga,
wellness services, and restorative activities.
The retreat's goal is to offer a peaceful diversion from the stress of everyday life,
giving people a place to re-establish their inner connections and promote general wellbeing. We explore the ageless concepts and old wisdom of yoga as we dig into its profound teachings behind these virtual walls.
Regardless of your level of experience,
our community extends a warm welcome to you as you take the mat for the first time.
Our community values diversity in the same way that yoga offers a variety of poses.
We honor people from all walks of life who have united to spread the life-changing benefits of yoga.
Think of this page as your haven for living intentionally.
Take part in talks, experiment with guided practices,
and lose yourself in the peace that yoga provides.
Permit this area to serve as a haven apart from the daily chaos.
